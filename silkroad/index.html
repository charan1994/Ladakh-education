Wonder activity

Write an essay of up to 500 words.


Imagine how your life would have been different without the Silk Road. What goods and services would be hard to find if it was not for the silk road? Would it impact your daily life? Why or why not? For this activity I would encourage you to walk around your neighborhood/locality and identify all the different goods that you might or might not have access to. This will help you think how certain goods and services in your community are available and wonder how life might have been different without them.
